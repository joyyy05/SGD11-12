SUSTAINABLE SOCIETY

What comes to mind when you hear the word sustainable? Is it the word livable? Or is it the word healthy? Perhaps safe? Well you're not wrong if you think about it, but sustainable or sustainability is the capability to maintain or uphold the balance or state of a being.

Now that you know what sustainable is, Do you think you are in a sustainable environtment? Look around and observe if your environment would be able to handle its own and sustain it's balance in the future or will it fall down to the waves of change and future disasters?

This is what we, The EcoTopia Members, aim to show and spread awareness of. Welcome to the Sustainable Society Chapter. In this chapter we talk about the SDGs covered, what the problem there is to address and what could be the solutions to the problems.

COVERED SDGs

Here are the SDGs covered in this chapter:

SDG #11.
The goal of this SDG is to make cities and other places safe, resilient, inclusive and sustainable.
SDG #12.
The goal of this SDG is to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.
